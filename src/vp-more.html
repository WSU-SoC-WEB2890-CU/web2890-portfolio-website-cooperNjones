VP Campaign (Mobile Only Site)

Objectives

For this website I was commisioned to design and program a marketing funnel/landing page for a Weber State candidate who was running for activities VP. The goal for this website was to make voting easily accesible for all the people she was talking to and making sharing and ease top priority.

Deliverables

Prototyping the landing page and getting revisions and updates from the client
All Images, videos, and content
A fully functional landing page matching campaign theme

Solutions

Having a QR code that others could scan to vote Matching the theme colors of the campaign so others could clearly distinguish the candidate Providing all info in a concise location for ease of access Designing purely for mobile
