My Work

Apollo Marketing

Apollo Marketing is a marketing agency that compensated a friend and I to make a marketing/sales funnel website that could properly schedule appointments as well as provide information regarding the company. This was a very exciting project to be a part of and required problem solving skills and communication with clientele.

VP Campaign

The VP Campaign website that I created was done as a side project for a student attending my local college. The student tasked me with making a MOBILE ONLY website that displayed her core values as well as had a QR code that linked students other students to her website so multiple people could view and share at the same time.

Photography Site

This was a personal product photography site that I built for a school project. The purpose was to give exposure to an individual for a service that they were providing as well as providing all contact information on the site to make it easy and efficient for the customer to reach out at any time. This site was a great representation of skills.

Budgeting App

This budgeting app was another school project using Python and Django. This was one of the more challenging applications that I built in my time in college due to the fact that it was almost all back-end programming and very minimal front-end design. Although this was challenging it helped me to better understand the backend.

Car Sales website

This car sales website was a school project that required me to design and build a car sales website using bootstrap, HTML, and CSS. It also required me to make my own color selections and change existing logos on the site to reflect the new colors that I had chosen. The site turned out very well and it looks really professional.

Sasquatch event form

Although this portfolio piece is probably my poorest looking, it presents my skills that I learned in Vue.js during a client side frameworks class that I took in college. It presents a form and then stores the information entered into a database to then output back to the user. Although this was also challenging it was good to learn.
