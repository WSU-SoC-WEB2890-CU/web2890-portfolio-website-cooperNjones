Developer

My development career started in college around 2020. I love design and think that being able to translate my designs into code makes me feel like I am contributing on another level. With my college experience I have worked on projects that had me deal with all kinds of languages and frameworks. Some of these include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Vue.js, Python, C++, Java, Python, Ruby, Django, Ruby on rails, SQL, and Bootstrap . I want to continue broadening my programming skills and want to pursue a career where it is still a vital thing in my day-to-day.

Designer

Design is the reason that I opted to gravitate towards the front end of the web industry. Since I was in Jr. High and experiencing Photoshop for the first time I have loved creating and discovering my own style and designs. Through trial and error over the last 8+ years I have loved discovering the creative process and have explored software such as Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Lightroom cc, Adobe XD, and Figma. I hope to continue to perfect my design skills, and want to tailor it towards companies who are looking for help with the appeal and functionality of the designs.

Creator

Being someone who loves to create something from the original idea all the way to the final deliverable, I have dabbled in all sorts of side projects over my life. These have provided an outlet for me to unleash and perfect my creative process. Some of the things that helped me make my creative skills more well rounded include professional photographer, professional videographer, content creator, Twitch streamer, YouTube video creator, and graphic design work. All of these things have helped me discover new and emerging software and I continue to pursue future interests such as animation and 3D.
